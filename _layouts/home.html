FRESH

The Fluent Resume and Employment System for Humans

A user-first, data-driven workflow, standard, and toolset for 21st century job candidates, employees, and career professionals.

{{ content }}

https://github.com/fresh-standard

What is FRESH? Three things.

  1. A workflow—a formal set of suggestions and best practices for resume authoring and career management (RACM).
  2. An open document schema establishing a universal source of truth for your resume, career, and identity data.
  3. A moderated toolchain of tried and true resume and document authoring tools from HackMyResume to pandoc.

FRESH is 100% open source and has no commercial or corporate affiliate or backing of any kind. While commercial tools and services are free to use FRESH under the MIT license, FRESH documents and workflows themselves are open, clean, standards-driven, and free of tool- or company-specific lock-in or cruft—today or ten years from today.
